Klaus Meldgård

Underviser, dressur (berider)

Klaus Meldgård, our accomplished Underviser in dressage, brings a wealth of expertise and passion to our team. As a seasoned berider, Klaus is dedicated to training both horses and riders, ensuring they reach their highest potential in the art of dressage.

Personality:

Klaus is known for his patience, precision, and unwavering commitment to excellence. His calm demeanor and methodical approach make him a respected mentor among his students. Klaus's deep understanding of horse behavior and his empathetic nature allow him to forge strong bonds with both his equine and human pupils, fostering an environment of mutual respect and trust.

History:

Klaus's journey in the world of dressage began at a young age, fueled by a lifelong passion for horses. After completing rigorous training and earning his credentials as a berider, he gained extensive experience in both national and international arenas. Klaus joined our team with a mission to share his knowledge and inspire the next generation of dressage riders. His legacy is marked by numerous successful students and horses that have thrived under his expert guidance.
