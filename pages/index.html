Vestbirk Sportsriding Center

Newly built riding center with state-of-the-art facilities and surroundings. At Vestbirk Sportsridecenter we can offer you and your horse everything in terms of stables, facility cards and training. In addition, we also rent out our facilities for conventions and other events.

Take a look around and see what we can offer.

Part of VMSE

Vestbirk Sportsridecenter is owned by Vestbirk Musik- & Sportsefterskole. That is why our riding center is always full of life with the students on the riding line.

Don't worry - we still have space for you as a private lodger.
