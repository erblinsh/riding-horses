Daisy

Daisy, adorned in a coat as pristine as freshly fallen snow, embodies the essence of purity and innocence. Her pure white fur, luminous and soft to the touch, blankets her slender frame with a brilliance that illuminates her surroundings. With delicate features and a gentle presence, Daisy radiates a timeless elegance that captivates all who behold her.

  • Breed: Sweeden
  • Age: 8 years old
  • Color: White
  • Markings: Pure, luminous, enchanting.

Personality:

Gentle and compassionate, Daisy offers comfort and support to all who cross his path.

History:

Born into a loving family, Daisy's gentle nature blossomed amidst tranquil countryside pastures.
