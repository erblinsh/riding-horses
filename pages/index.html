Vestbirk Sportsriding Center

Newly built riding center with state-of-the-art facilities and surroundings. At Vestbirk Sportsridecenter we can offer you and your horse everything in terms of stables, facility cards and training. In addition, we also rent out our facilities for conventions and other events.

Take a look around and see what we can offer.

Part of VMSE

Vestbirk Sportsridecenter is owned by Vestbirk Musik- & Sportsefterskole. That is why our riding center is always full of life with the students on the riding line.

Don't worry - we still have space for you as a private lodger.

Facilities

Equestrian tracks

2 indoor riding arenas with Walber ebb/flood fiber bottom. The courses follow the international goals of 20×60 and 30×70.

In addition, outdoor show jumping and 2x dressage arenas.

Planting area

Several stalls and 2 water fountains, located in the stalls and directly opposite the saddle rooms.

Stalling

Our 84 Röwer & Rüb horse boxes are divided into 2 connected halls with easy access to both the boarding area and the riding halls.

Local area

Vestbirk Sportsridecenter is located in the middle of Søhøjlandet and its magnificent nature, with direct access to the nature trail from the grounds of the riding center.
