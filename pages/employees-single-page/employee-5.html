Erling Naamann

Stable staff

Erling Naamann is a distinguished Underviser in dressage, renowned for his expertise as a berider. Sune's exceptional skills in training and his deep understanding of dressage techniques make him an invaluable asset to our team.

Personality:

Sune is characterized by his dedication, precision, and approachable nature. He is a patient and insightful instructor who takes great pride in fostering a positive learning environment. Sune's ability to connect with both horses and riders on a personal level ensures that each training session is productive and enjoyable, promoting mutual growth and respect.

History:

Sune's passion for dressage began early in his life, leading him to pursue extensive training and certification as a berider. Over the years, he has honed his skills through a combination of hands-on experience and competitive success. Sune has trained under some of the most respected names in the field, further enhancing his techniques and methodologies. Since joining our team, he has been instrumental in guiding numerous horses and riders to achieve excellence in dressage, earning widespread admiration and respect within the community.
