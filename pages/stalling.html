The best surroundings

With our large, bright Röwer & Rüb bamboo horse boxes, your horse gets an environment with calm and relaxing surroundings.

The stable halls are large with plenty of walking space and a good indoor climate, which makes it nice for both you and your horse to move around in the hall.

In addition, there is direct access to the storage halls from the unloading area, where it is possible to park directly up to.
