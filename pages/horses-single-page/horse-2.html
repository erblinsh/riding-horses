Midnight

Midnight is a stunning stallion with a coat that seems to absorb the darkness of the night sky. His rich brown hue, reminiscent of polished mahogany, is accented by sleek black points that add to his striking appearance.

  • Breed: Germany
  • Age: 5 years old
  • Color: Dark bay
  • Markings: Black mane, tail, and legs

Personality:

Midnight is a mysterious stallion with a gentle disposition. Despite his imposing appearance, he's remarkably affectionate and intelligent, captivating everyone with his calm demeanor and keen awareness.

History:

Born under a moonlit sky, Midnight was destined for greatness. Raised in a family of esteemed performance horses, he quickly proved himself in dressage and jumping, earning a reputation as a rising star in the equestrian world.
